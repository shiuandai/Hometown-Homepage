Visit Taipei

Enjoy the culture and architecture in this vibrant city of Taiwan.

Top three activities to do at Taipei

Enjoy the Architectural Landmark of Taipei City

Chiang Kai-shek Memorial Hall is a great place to spend your morning. The place is enriched with the beauty of traditional Chinese gardens. There are various activities taking place in the square.

Visit Jiufen old street in northeast of Taiwan

You can spend a whole day to enjoy the local culture in the old street. Along the street there are shops vending the most famous country snack of Jiufen, yam dish and various local dishes.

Go for a local market to enjoy the food

Spend your night time enjoying the market activities. Night markets can pique your interest, with streets filled with aromas of spices and an assortment of goods.
